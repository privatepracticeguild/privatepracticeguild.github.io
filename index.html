<PRIVATE PRACTICE>

Private Practice is a semi-casual progressive guild that enjoys playing all aspects of the game with heroic-mythic raiding as a primary focus. Many of us also enjoy pushing keys as well. While we take progrssion content seriously, we prioritize the group experience above all else and like to keep a low stress atmosphere. We're a pretty comical, chill group of people aging from mid 20s to mid 30s. Some of us have known each other for 10 years and some for just 10 days! We like to let loose and laugh, give each other a hard time, help each other out and run keys to help gear mains, alts or just for fun of course! We have a great game-life balance, understanding that most of us have fulltime jobs, families and actual lives. You can find people online just about any time of the day with evenings and weekends being the most active.
